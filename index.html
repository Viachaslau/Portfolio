About Me

Started to learn back-end development just to take up an in-demand occupation I have found myself (to my surprise!) fascinated by the creation of truly complex systems. It was no longer enough for me to just do controller programming, and I wanted to really immerse myself in the world of back-end development. Until recently, I had experience in programming of microcontrollers. But the connection of the chip to the server and the creation of the user interface is what really interested me.

One of great merits of back-end development for me - you will never be bored with it. It's like a big puzzle you're grappling with, that makes you think about it day and night. And what's the most important - through solving these diverse problems back-end development fosters long-term learning and honing my skills.

If I'm not coding, you can find me discovering new amazing spots near my home or far away. Or riding my favorite motorcycle. Or skiing high up in mountains. Or just reading a book lying in the bed. Oh, I almost forgot, I developing home chargers for electric cars, and, in my immodest opinion, I'm good enough at it.
